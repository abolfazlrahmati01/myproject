    Our proprietary platform is a simple-to-use system for trading in cryptocurrencies.
    Our customer defines their goals and trading parameters and the system does the

    AITrades customers don’t need in-depth experience in the cryptocurrency market
    to begin using our platform. The simple to use program allows our customers to
    start using the tool immediately no complicated learning curve.
